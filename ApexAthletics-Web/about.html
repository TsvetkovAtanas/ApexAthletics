Facility & Business Growth

2005 - 2008

First Steps

Opened the first gym in Plovdiv with just 2,000 sq. ft. of space. Expanded the gym to include a space for group fitness classes.

2010 - 2015

Expansion

Opened a second branch in Sofia, doubling the size of the gym’s footprint. Upgraded all locations with state-of-the-art fitness equipment.

2020 - 2025

Digital Masters

Introduced virtual classes and an app for online bookings. Launched a premium personal training studio and celebrated 20 years in business.

Community & Achievements

2006 - 2010

Closer to the Community

Hosted the first “Charity Fitness Challenge,” raising funds for local schools. Introduced community yoga programs in local parks.

2015 - 2018

Fitness for Everyone

Awarded “Best Gym in Plovdiv” by the local magazines. Reached 5,000 active members and made free youth fitness classes.

2021 - 2023

Make the World a Better Place

Organized free fitness workshops for workers during the pandemic. Earned a national award for “Outstanding Contribution to Wellness.”
